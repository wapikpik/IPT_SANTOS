Mt. Tapulao

Mount Tapulao, the highest peak in Zambales at 2,037 meters, offers challenging hikes through forests, grasslands, and rocky terrain. Known for its scenic views and diverse ecosystems, it attracts trekkers seeking a tough climb and panoramic vistas of Zambales, nearby mountains, and the coastline.

Mt Pulag

Mount Pulag, located in the Cordillera Mountains, is the third-highest peak in the Philippines at 2,922 meters. Known for its stunning sea of clouds and diverse ecosystems, it offers a challenging hike through mossy forests, grasslands, and alpine meadows. It's a popular destination for trekkers and nature enthusiasts.

Mt. Batulao

Mount Batulao, located in Batangas, stands at 811 meters above sea level. Known for its scenic views, grassy slopes, and moderate difficulty, it's a popular hiking destination for beginners and experienced trekkers alike. The summit offers panoramic views of Batangas, Cavite, and nearby mountains, making it a favorite trail.
