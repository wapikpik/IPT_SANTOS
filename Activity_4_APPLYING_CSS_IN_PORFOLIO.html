MT. PULAG

Mt. Pulag, the third highest peak in the Philippines, is renowned for its breathtaking sea of clouds and stunning sunrise views. Located in the Cordillera mountain range, it offers diverse flora and fauna, cool temperatures, and a challenging yet rewarding hike, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventurers

MT. TAPULAO

Mt. Tapulao, located in Zambales, Philippines, is known for its rugged terrain and cooler climate due to its elevation of 2,037 meters. Its name, meaning "many pine trees," reflects the lush pine forests near the summit. The hike offers scenic views, making it a popular trekking destination.

MT. BATULAO

Mt. Batulao, located in Nasugbu, Batangas, is a popular hiking destination known for its rolling hills and scenic views. Rising 811 meters above sea level, its name comes from "Bato sa Ilaw" (rock illuminated by light), reflecting its picturesque sunsets. The trail offers beginner-friendly yet exciting challenges.
